The Story

The sari-sari store spirit, reimagined.

The sari-sari store has always been the heartbeat of the Filipino neighborhood — a place where you could find a bit of everything and always be greeted with a smile. Growing up, it was where we ran errands for nanay, where we spent our baon, where neighbors became friends over small bags of chichirya and cold softdrinks.

"We wanted to take that spirit and package it for the world."

At Sari-Sari Box, our promise is simple: Open. Smile. Repeat. We handle the sourcing and packing, so all you have to do is unbox your favorites and enjoy the taste of home — whether you're in Taguig or Tokyo, Makati or Madrid.

Meet the Founder

Hi! I'm Geanlijah.

As someone who deeply values our local culinary traditions, I started this journey to share the comfort of home-grown snacks with fellow Filipinos abroad and curious food enthusiasts everywhere.

Growing up surrounded by the sights, sounds, and flavors of Filipino snack culture, I always knew that our local products deserved a bigger stage. From the crispy crunch of Chippy to the sweet nostalgia of ChocNut — every item in our boxes carries a story I want to share with the world.

Driven by a passion for convenience and quality, I personally curate every box to ensure that each delivery feels like a warm hug from home. Because that's what we Filipinos do — we share what we love.
